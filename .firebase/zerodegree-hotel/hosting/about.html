About Us

Your Ideal Living Solution

Where Luxury Meets Comfort and Every Stay is an Experience.

Our Mission

To provide personalized services that cater to the unique needs of every guest and create a welcoming environment where comfort, style, and sophistication harmonize.

Our Vision

To be the benchmark of excellence in hospitality, inspiring our guests to create lasting memories with every stay.

At ZeroDegree Hotel, we redefine hospitality with a blend of world-class amenities, exceptional service, and a commitment to creating unforgettable experiences. Nestled in the heart of Port Harcourt, our hotel is the epitome of luxury, offering a serene escape for travelers seeking relaxation, adventure, or both.

Our History

Founded in 1990, ZeroDegree Hotel started with a vision to redefine luxury hospitality. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted brand known for impeccable service, innovative designs, and a deep commitment to sustainability..

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We are committed to eco-friendly living, integrating green design and energy-efficient practices in every apartment.

Innovation

We embrace modern technology and smart solutions to enhance comfort, convenience, and security for our residents.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, from our luxury apartments to our personalized services, ensuring an exceptional living experience.
